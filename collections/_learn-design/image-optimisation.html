Optimising images can yield some of largest byte savings and performance improvements for your agency website.

Image Optimisation Checklist

Image optimisation is both an art and a science as there is no definitive answer for how to best compress an image and there are many well-developed tools that can help reduce the size of an image significantly.

Here are some tips for you to get started:

  • Prefer vector formats: Vector images are resolution and scale independent, which makes them a perfect fit for multi-device and high-resolution world.
  • Pick best raster image format: Determine functional requirements and select the one that suits each particular asset.
  • Experiment with optimal quality settings for raster formats: Try with the lower quality settings, the results are often very good and byte savings are significant.
  • Serve scaled images: Resize images on the server and ensure that the display size is as close as possible to the natural size of an image.
